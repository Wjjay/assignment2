Global Warming: An Artificial Disaster

Although the idea of economic propserity sounds nice to us as a species, we tend to ignore some of the side effects. It has been incredibly taxing on the environment as countries continue to industrialize, releasing immense quantities of carbon dioxide into the atmosphere and trapping in the sun's heat.

If we continue to ignore the climate crisis, our future will be bleak. On land, we have observed some of the worst bushfires in recent history. In the ocean, coral reefs are dying at an unprecedented rate as increasing water temperatures are causing them to bleach out. This is just the tip of the iceberg.
